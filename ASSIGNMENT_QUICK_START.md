## English

### For Dispatcher/Owner: How to Assign a Load to a Driver

**Step 1: Login to TMS**
- Open TMS application
- Login with Dispatcher/Owner credentials

**Step 2: Navigate to Loads**
- Click "Loads" in the sidebar
- Find the load you want to assign
- Load status must be "Booked" or "Rate Confirmed"

**Step 3: Click Assign Button**
- Find the load in the list
- Click the green **"Assign"** button
- Assignment dialog opens

**Step 4: Select Driver, Truck, Trailer**
- **Select Driver**: Choose from active drivers in dropdown
- **Select Truck**: Choose an available truck
- **Select Trailer**: Choose an available trailer
- All three are required

**Step 5: Confirm Assignment**
- Click **"Confirm"** button
- System assigns the load
- **Automatic notification sent to driver**

**What Happens Next:**
тЬЕ Load status changes to "Assigned"  
тЬЕ Driver, Truck, Trailer status change to "On Trip"  
тЬЕ Driver receives notification immediately  
тЬЕ Driver has **24 hours to accept or reject**

---

### For Driver: How to Respond to Assignment

**Step 1: Login to Driver App**
- Open TMS Driver App
- Login with your driver credentials

**Step 2: View Pending Assignments**
- Go to your **Driver Dashboard**
- Look for **"Pending Load Assignments"** card at the top
- This shows all loads assigned to you waiting for response

**Step 3: Review the Assignment**
Card shows:
- Load Number (e.g., "LGP-2024-001")
- Origin and Destination (e.g., "Los Angeles тЖТ Phoenix")
- Pickup Date
- Assigned Truck (e.g., "TK-001")
- Assigned Trailer (e.g., "TR-001")
- тП▒я╕П **24 hours to respond**

**Step 4: Make Your Decision**

#### Option A: ACCEPT the Load
- Click **green "Accept" button**
- System accepts immediately
- тЬЕ Load moves to "Current Load" section
- тЬЕ You can now start the trip
- тЬЕ Dispatcher is notified you accepted

#### Option B: REJECT the Load
- Click **red "Reject" button**
- Dialog opens asking "Why are you rejecting?"
- Enter reason (optional):
  - "Truck not suitable for this route"
  - "Not enough rate for distance"
  - "My truck is having issues"
  - Any other reason
- Click **"Reject"** to confirm
- тЬЕ Load goes back to "Available" status
- тЬЕ Dispatcher sees your rejection and reason
- тЬЕ Can reassign to another driver

**After 24 Hours:**
- If you don't respond, assignment expires
- Load goes back to "Available"
- Dispatcher can reassign to someone else

## FAQ

**Q: What if I accidentally reject a load?**
A: Contact your dispatcher. They can reassign it to you again.

**Q: Can I change my decision after accepting?**
A: Once accepted, you start the trip workflow. Contact your dispatcher for special cases.

**Q: Why is the assignment showing as expired?**
A: You had 24 hours to respond. Assignment auto-expired. Dispatcher can reassign.

**Q: What if I don't see the pending assignments card?**
A: Refresh the page. If still not showing, contact support.

**Q: Can the dispatcher assign a load after I reject it?**
A: Yes! Dispatcher can immediately reassign the load to another driver.
